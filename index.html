Referring a Patient
Takes Three Steps

1

Call or Email Us

Contact our dispatch line at 571-604-8137 or email info@abyssinianco.com. Our team responds within one business hour.

2

Share Patient & Trip Details

Provide the patient's name, pickup address, destination, appointment time, mobility needs, and Medicaid ID (if applicable). We handle authorization.

3

We Confirm & Dispatch

You'll receive a confirmation with driver details. On the day of transport, we keep you updated on pickup and drop-off times.
