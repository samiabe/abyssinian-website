For healthcare providers

Referring a Patient Is Simple

We work directly with hospital case managers, discharge planners, and Medicaid coordinators. Our process is fast and reliable.

1

Call or Email Us

Contact us with the patient's pickup location, destination, date, time, and any mobility needs. We respond within 2 hours.

2

We Confirm the Trip

We verify insurance or Medicaid authorization, assign a driver, and send you a confirmation with driver details.

3

Patient Gets There

Our driver arrives on time, assists the patient, and delivers them safely. You can track the trip status by phone.
